Our Story

Born from the Çarshia's Heartbeat

Kafe Çarshia opened its doors in the old bazaar of Prishtina — a place where traders have gathered for centuries. We honour that spirit by bringing together the world's finest green beans with the craft of Kosovo's growing barista culture.

We roast in-house every Tuesday and Friday. Come on those mornings and the whole neighbourhood smells like a promise.
